How ReconPad works

From two CSVs to a clean reconciliation
in under 5 minutes

Step 1

Upload two files

Drop your bank statement CSV and your accounting export (QBO, Xero, Wave, whatever). No integrations, no OAuth, no setup. Just files.

Step 2

Auto-match fires instantly

Our fuzzy matching engine pairs transactions by amount, date, and description. 90–95% of transactions match automatically. You see the confirmation in one click.

Step 3

Review only the exceptions

The discrepancy queue shows only transactions that didn't match — annotate timing differences, flag bank errors, and export a signed-off audit trail PDF.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Works with any CSV

Chase, BofA, Wells, Stripe, QBO, Xero, Wave, FreshBooks — if it exports CSV, ReconPad reads it. Smart column detection handles messy headers automatically.

Multi-client workspace

Bookkeepers juggling 20+ clients see each account's last reconciled date at a glance. Monthly recon history stored per client, always findable.

Audit-trail PDF export

Every reconciliation produces a timestamped PDF: matched items, unmatched items, annotations, and a running variance — ready to file or send to a client.

Live variance counter

See the difference between your bank balance and book balance update in real time as you work. You know you're balanced before you're even done.

Inline annotations

Add notes to timing differences, bank charges, or reconciling items right in the interface. All notes carry through to the export — no sticky note required.

No integrations needed

No bank logins, no accounting API keys, no permissions to manage. Upload CSVs, work locally in your browser, done. Your data never touches a third-party server.
