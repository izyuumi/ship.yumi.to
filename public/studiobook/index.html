How StudioBook works

Three minutes of setup. Zero spreadsheets. Clarity on day one.

🎹

Add your students

Create a card for each student: name, instrument, lesson day/time, duration, and your rate. Done in 30 seconds per student.

✅

Log each lesson in one tap

After every lesson, tap: Attended, Cancelled — makeup owed, or Makeup used. That's it. Credits are tracked automatically.

📝

Add a quick lesson note

After each session, jot one or two sentences: what you worked on, what to practice, where to pick up next time. Never walk into a lesson blind again.

💰

Bill confidently at month-end

StudioBook shows you exactly what each family owes: lessons attended × rate, minus any makeup credits applied. One-click copy to paste into a Venmo request or email.

Everything a private studio needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Student roster

Makeup credit tracking
