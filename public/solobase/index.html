How SoloBase Works

One client. One place. Start to finish.

From the first proposal to the final paid invoice, every step of your client relationship lives in a single, searchable workspace.

Send a Proposal

Build a polished, branded proposal from a template. Share a link — the client reads it in their browser, approves with one click, no account needed.

Get the Contract Signed

SoloBase auto-generates a contract from your approved proposal. Client e-signs directly in their browser. Legally binding, stored permanently in your workspace.

Invoice & Get Paid

Create an invoice in seconds from the signed contract. Accept card or ACH payments. Automatic late payment reminders — no more chasing checks.

Build Your Client History

Every proposal, contract, invoice, and note for a client lives on their profile page. When they come back six months later, you'll know exactly what you quoted them and why.
