From data to draft in minutes

Tell IEPScribe about your student. Get a complete, compliant, editable IEP draft. Export and you're done.

1

Enter student performance data

Input assessment scores, teacher observations, current performance notes, and eligibility category. Takes 5 minutes — no special format required.

2

AI generates your PLAAFP + goals

IEPScribe writes the Present Level narrative in professional, IEP-compliant language. Then it generates 3–6 SMART annual goals with benchmarks — customized to your student's data, not a template.

3

Review, edit, and finalize

You stay in control. Edit anything inline — change wording, add goals, adjust baselines. IEPScribe is a powerful starting point, not a black box. You always review before it's final.

4

Export and write progress notes in 30 seconds

Export to PDF or Word for your district system. At progress report time, mark each goal's progress and IEPScribe writes the quarterly note. No more weekend marathons.
