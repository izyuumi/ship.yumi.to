Sample generated note

progress_note_session_47.pdf
SUBJECTIVE:

Client presented with moderate anxiety (self-reported 6/10). Reports increased work stress over past week; sleep disrupted 3–4 nights. Denied SI/HI. Mood mildly dysthymic. Reports completing journaling homework 4 of 7 days.

OBJECTIVE:

Client was alert and oriented x3. Speech normal rate and rhythm. Affect congruent to reported mood. No psychomotor agitation observed.

ASSESSMENT:

Client continues to meet criteria for GAD (F41.1). Anxiety symptoms persist but slight improvement in functional coping from prior session. CBT skills showing early adoption.

PLAN:

Continue weekly individual therapy (90837). Practiced cognitive restructuring re: work stress. Assigned thought record worksheet for perceived catastrophizing. Next session in 1 week.
