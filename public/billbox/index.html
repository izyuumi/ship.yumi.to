The problem

Invoices arrive from everywhere: PDFs, email threads, spreadsheets, random portals. Then, once a month, someone plays detective—searching attachments, copying totals, categorizing spend, and hoping nothing was missed.

It’s slow
Manual digging turns into a recurring project.
It’s fragile
Miss one invoice → wrong numbers, wrong decisions.
It’s invisible
No real-time view of vendor spend or renewals.
