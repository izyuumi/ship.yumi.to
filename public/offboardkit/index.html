Frequently Asked Questions

Does this replace our ITSM tool? ▾
No — OffboardKit is a focused tool for employee lifecycle checklists. If you already use FreshService or Jira and love it, you can keep using those for general tickets. OffboardKit fills the specific gap of role-based, auditable onboarding/offboarding that most ITSM tools do poorly for small teams.
Does it integrate with our apps (Slack, Microsoft 365, etc.)? ▾
At launch, OffboardKit focuses on checklist management and reminders — the human side of offboarding. Automation integrations (auto-disabling accounts) are on our roadmap. Early access users will vote on which integrations we build first.
What if I'm an MSP managing multiple clients? ▾
MSP support is a priority. You'll be able to manage separate playbooks and runbooks per client from one dashboard. Tell us about your MSP workflow when you sign up.
Does this work for onboarding too? ▾
Yes — OffboardKit supports both onboarding and offboarding playbooks. Same role-based templates, same assignment and reminder logic, but in reverse. New hire day 1 setup becomes a structured, trackable process too.
