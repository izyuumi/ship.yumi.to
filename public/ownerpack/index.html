The problem

Property management software is great at operations. It’s not great at owner-ready reporting. So you export, paste, reformat, screenshot, attach photos, and rebuild the same report packet over and over.

“Every owner wants something slightly different.”
One-page P&L, maintenance photos per line item, monthly comps…
“The canned statement is fine for 3 owners.”
The rest becomes an Excel weekend.
It doesn’t scale past 40–100 doors.
You either hire for reporting, or burn out.
You’re afraid to push back.
Big accounts ask for more — and you don’t want to lose them.

The solution

OwnerPack turns your exports into a system: templates, owner rules, and auto-generated packets. Same underlying data — delivered in the format each owner expects.

  • Template once (P&L-only, P&L + maintenance, full packet).
  • Assign per owner (photos on/off, detail level, notes).
  • Generate all packets at once with a review step.
  • Send + track delivery so nothing is missed.
