How FirstDay works

Set it up once.
Run every hire on autopilot.

From offer acceptance to the end of week one — fully tracked, fully coordinated, without you as the middleman.

1

Pick a role template — or build your own

Start with a pre-built playbook for Software Engineer, Sales Rep, Customer Support, or Operations. Each step is pre-filled with suggested tasks, owners, and timing. Customize in minutes — not hours.

🧑‍💻 Engineer 📞 Sales Rep 🎧 Customer Support ⚙️ Operations + custom
2

Assign tasks across teams — once

Each step belongs to someone specific. IT gets "Provision laptop + email + Slack." The manager gets "Schedule 30-day check-in." HR gets "Send benefits enrollment." Everyone knows exactly what they own.

IT Provision email, Slack, GitHub access Day -2
Mgr Schedule 1:1 intro + 30-day check-in Day 1
HR Send benefits enrollment + I-9 docs Day 1
3

New hire gets their own clean portal

Your new hire receives a single link — their personal onboarding portal. Sign documents, watch the welcome video, fill out their emergency contact, complete their own checklist. No login required, no app to install.

4

FirstDay chases people — so you don't have to

Overdue tasks trigger automatic escalating reminders. First a gentle nudge via email. Then a flag in your HR dashboard. You see what's complete and what's blocked in one glance — and you only intervene when something actually needs you.
