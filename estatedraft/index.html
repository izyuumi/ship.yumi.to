How EstateDraft works

From blank intake to signed-ready package in under 10 minutes.

1

Complete the guided client intake

Fill in a single structured form: client info, family details, assets, executor choices, healthcare preferences, beneficiaries. Conditional logic hides fields that don't apply — no confusing blank fields for childless clients or single individuals.

2

EstateDraft generates the full package

In seconds, EstateDraft produces a complete document set: Last Will & Testament, Durable Power of Attorney, Healthcare Directive / Living Will, and optionally a Revocable Living Trust. Every field filled. Pronouns correct. Conditional clauses applied.

3

Review and refine in the editor

Attorney or paralegal reviews the document in a clean browser editor. Make any changes — update one field and it updates everywhere automatically. No more hunting through Word docs.

4

Export a professional PDF bundle

Download a beautifully formatted PDF package, ready to send to the client for review and execution. Client history is stored — run an update any time they have a life change.
