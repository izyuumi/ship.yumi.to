How ShiftLog works

Three simple steps. Works on any phone. No training required.

Step 1

Outgoing shift logs the handoff

Before clocking out, staff fills a short structured form: what got done, what's still pending, any issues to flag, and urgent alerts. Takes 2 minutes.

Step 2

Incoming shift gets notified

The next crew receives an automatic push notification with the handoff summary. Urgent flags appear in red at the top so nothing critical gets buried.

Step 3

Required acknowledgment ‚Äî no excuses

Incoming staff must tap "Got it" to confirm they've read the handoff. Manager dashboard shows who has and hasn't acknowledged. "I didn't know" is now impossible.
