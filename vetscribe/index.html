Built for how vets actually work

Not a transcription service. Not a human-medicine tool with a vet skin. VetScribe is purpose-built for veterinary practice.

Species-aware terminology

Canine, feline, equine, avian, exotic — VetScribe knows the right terminology for each species. No more correcting "patient" to "patient (canine)" or fixing breed-inappropriate drug doses.

Quick-tap exam templates

Tap to select findings from common PE templates by species. Normal findings auto-populate, you only note deviations — the same mental shorthand you already use in your head.

Discharge instructions included

From the same case input, generate client-facing discharge instructions in plain language. Medication schedule, activity restrictions, warning signs to watch — all pre-written and editable.

No PIMS integration required

Works alongside whatever software your clinic uses. No IT project, no vendor contract, no 6-month implementation. Open VetScribe, fill in 5 fields, copy the note. That's it.

Works on mobile too

Draft a note between appointments, or quickly generate a note on your phone from the break room. Your notes sync so you can review and copy from any device.

Your notes, your style

VetScribe learns your phrasing preferences over time. Keep full control — every generated note is yours to edit before it ever goes into a record.
